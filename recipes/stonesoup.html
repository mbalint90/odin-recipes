Stone soup

Description

This is my go to recipe everytime I'm coming home from a war and I have nothing to eat.

Works best with quality stone!

Ingredients

  • 1 piece quality stone
  • Water
  • Whatever is in your host's pantry
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Get some water boiling
  2. Wash the stone carefully then place it in the boiling water
  3. Convience your host to give the rest of the ingredients to you
  4. Put whatever you managed to get inside the pot
  5. Season the soup with salt and pepper
  6. Enjoy!
